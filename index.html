Gumi the Cat

While in the wake of her breakthrough success of her debut, Gumi lent her vocals to artists Flume, Skrillex, and Calvin Harris which delivered more of her signature mix of cool synth pop and airy melodies.

This Korean artist who was raised in Japan resides in Los Angeles. Her album hit number one on U.S. Billboards and she headlined her second concert tour with FKA Twigs. A few months later, the more lavish-sounding singles and collaborations with Arca, Dev Hynes, Sampha, and Paul Epworth, brought on a popular album which gave her critical acclaim in the Electronic world.

Dezi Love

This pop, punk, and alternative rock princess who is known for her edgy look and artistic tattoo vibes started her career in early 1990’s. While playing at a party in a band she was discovered by young musicians from a SKA band. She was immediately asked to make a solo album featuring popular groups such as Sum 41, Nofx, and No Doubt. She became known as the Emo Pop Queen because of her single, “It's too late.”

She continues to rock out alternative grooves with punk instrumentals and emo lyrical compilations.
